Curriculum Vitae

I am a PhD student at the Government department at Harvard. My research focuses on inequality, representation and migration. Methodolologically, I rely on causal inference in observational settings. I am also a research fellow in the Multidisciplinary Program in Inequality and Social Policy at the Harvard Kennedy School, and a visiting researcher at WZB Berlin.
